3D Synopsis

In this work, we propose a novel 3D route visualization tool, which aims to produce a trip synopsis of a prescribed length that is as informative and interesting as possible.

Intrinsic Symmetry

Enhancing the self-symmetry of a shape is of fundamental aesthetic virtue. In this work, we are interested in recovering the aesthetics of intrinsic symmetries, where an asymmetric shape is symmetrized while keeping its general pose and perceived dynamics.

Biography

    Graduated from Software Academy of SiChuan University in 2012. My undergraduate thesis which is related about "street view" guided by Prof. Mei Hong who is the dean of our academy.
